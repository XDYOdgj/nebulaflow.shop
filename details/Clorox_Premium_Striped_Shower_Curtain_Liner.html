Clorox Premium Striped Shower Curtain Liner

Upgrade your bathroom with this stylish and functional shower curtain liner that blends seamlessly with any decor. Designed with a bold striped pattern in multiple color options, it adds a modern aesthetic to your space while offering essential privacy. The waterproof PEVA material ensures easy cleaning and long-lasting performance, keeping your bathroom both dry and fresh.

Waterproof Design: The PEVA material provides excellent water resistance, making cleaning quick and hassle-free.
