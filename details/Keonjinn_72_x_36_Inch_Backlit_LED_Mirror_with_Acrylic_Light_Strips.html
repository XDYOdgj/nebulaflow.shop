Keonjinn 72 x 36 LED Mirror – Modern, Stylish, and Functional

Elevate your bathroom with the Keonjinn Backlit Mirror, blending form and function to suit your everyday needs.

Aesthetic Meets Innovation

This LED bathroom mirror features sleek acrylic wrapped light strips that deliver an ultra-modern look. With 120 LEDs per meter, it offers a cool white (6000K) backlight that enhances your bathroom’s ambiance and makes grooming tasks easier and more accurate. The design ensures no exposed wiring, keeping your space clean and organized.

High-Quality Glass for Clarity

Crafted with 5mm HD tempered glass, the mirror provides crystal-clear reflections that are distortion-free. This premium glass is not only safe but also built to last, making it suitable for wet and humid environments like showers or bathrooms with steam.

Customizable and Convenient Lighting

The mirror is equipped with a stepless dimming function and memory feature, allowing you to store your preferred brightness settings for quick access. Whether you're in a hurry or want to relax, the lighting can be adjusted to match your mood and needs.

Simple Installation for Any Setup

Designed for versatility, the mirror offers flexible installation options. It can be mounted on the wall either horizontally or vertically and supports both plug-in and hardwired setups. This makes it easy to fit into any bathroom design or layout.

Stay Clear, Stay Comfortable

The built-in anti-fog feature ensures your mirror remains clear and free from mist, even in the most humid conditions. Combined with its IP44 waterproof rating, it's a reliable choice for daily use that adds both style and safety to your bathroom space.

Upgrade your bathroom with a mirror that combines advanced technology, elegant design, and practical functionality.
